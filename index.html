ABOUT US

We Centroid Polymer Technologies located atKINFRA Integrated Industrial & Textile Park which is 30 km away from Coimbatore

The KINFRA Park at Palakkad,India hosts the first major defence production unit in Kerala. The factory site has the locational and economical advantages being strategically located in the state with proximity to sea port, rail connectivity and airport access for ease of logistics and this position allows us to capitalise on a solid delivery infrastructure.

We provide shorter lead times for 'at speed' and 'on time' deliveries to minimisethe impact on your businesses. We respond with full design support for your multi-discipline engineering teams We reduce the time for new part introduction and improve the 'time to market' for your products to your customers. We work with you to achieve your budget targets for the lifecycle cost of your product whilst minimising commercial risk to your business. And we give you instant access to our technical, project and customer service team
